Who We Are

Founded in 2018 in the heart of Galle, Smart Books Lanka began as a modest storefront with a singular mission: to make quality books accessible to every reader in the community. What started small has grown into the region's most trusted bookstore.

We serve students, parents, and avid readers alike — stocking everything from O/L and A/L textbooks to bestselling novels, illustrated children's books, and premium stationery essentials. Every shelf is curated with care.

Now, as we step into the digital age, we're bringing that same warmth and quality to readers across all of Sri Lanka — with an online store coming soon.
